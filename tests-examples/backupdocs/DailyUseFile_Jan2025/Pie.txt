 - townhall/ Health card need to be updated
Q: Documents required in case of switch
 - Two option either next company apply for highly qualified permit or
 - employee can also apply via Lawyer (costs appx 1000)
 - Document req: Legal owner's Power of Attorney , TIE card, Tax No and SEPE Contract
   between company and employee
 - If we already have a valid work permit, its easy to add another company to it.

 - Within one month current company inform Govt. So we should update TIE card.
 - TIE always linked to one company and has to be updated on switch.
